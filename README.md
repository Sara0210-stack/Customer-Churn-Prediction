# Banking Customer Analytics & Churn Risk Insights

## Overview
This project focuses on analyzing banking customer data to identify patterns, understand customer behavior, and predict churn risk. The goal is to derive actionable insights that can help improve customer retention and support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze customer data to identify trends and patterns  
- Detect factors contributing to customer churn  
- Build predictive models to estimate churn risk  
- Provide insights to support business strategies  

---

## Dataset
The dataset includes customer-related information such as:
- Demographics (age, geography, gender)  
- Account details (balance, tenure, number of products)  
- Activity indicators (credit card usage, active status)  
- Churn label (whether the customer exited or not)  

---

## Key Steps

### 1. Data Preprocessing
- Handled missing values and cleaned data  
- Converted categorical variables into numerical formats  
- Scaled and prepared data for modeling  

---

### 2. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
- Analyzed customer distribution across different segments  
- Identified trends in churn behavior  
- Visualized relationships between features and churn  

---

### 3. Feature Analysis
- Identified important variables influencing churn  
- Observed patterns such as:
  - Customers with lower activity are more likely to churn  
  - Certain regions show higher churn rates  
  - Balance and tenure impact retention  

---

### 4. Model Building
- Built classification models to predict churn:
  - Logistic Regression  
  - Random Forest  
- Evaluated models using:
  - Accuracy  
  - Precision & Recall  
  - Confusion Matrix  

---

### 5. Insights & Findings
- Customer activity is a strong indicator of churn  
- Customers with fewer products are more likely to leave  
- Certain demographic segments show higher churn risk  
- Model predictions can help identify high-risk customers  

---

## Results
- Successfully built models to predict customer churn  
- Identified key factors affecting customer retention  
- Generated insights useful for improving customer engagement strategies
